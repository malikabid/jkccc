Makhdoom Sahab Ropeway

Project Introduction

The Makhdoom Sahab Ropeway is a modern cable car system located in Srinagar, providing easy access to the revered shrine of Makhdoom Sahab. It offers a unique experience for pilgrims and tourists alike.

Project Features

  • Convenient access to Makhdoom Sahab shrine
  • Modern and safe cabins
  • Scenic views of Srinagar
  • Popular among pilgrims and tourists
