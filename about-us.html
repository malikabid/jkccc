About Us

JKCCC established in the year 1988, since then JKCCC has been a premier organization in developing and operationalising ropeways in Jammu and Kashmir.

JKCCC has been instrumental in developing the Gulmarg Gondola, the highest ropeway in the world, which has been a major tourist attraction in the region.

JKCCC has also developed the Makhdum Sahab Ropeway (MSRP), Jammu Ropeway (JRP), Pahalgama SKI lift, and Sonamarg SKI Draglift.

JKCCC has been a pioneer in developing ropeways in the region and has been a major contributor to the tourism industry in Jammu and Kashmir.
