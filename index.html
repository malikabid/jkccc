J&K Cable Car Corporation

JKCCC has been established in the year 1988. Since then JKCCC has been a premier organization in developing and operationalising ropeways in Jammu and Kashmir. The copration was started with an initial capital of Rs. 5000.00 Lacs and a paid up capital of Rs. 2357.00 Lacs

The company has total 235700 shares of Rs.1000 per share out of which 01 share each has been allotted in favor of Commissioner / Secretary Tourism, Director General Tourism, Managing Director JKCCC and rest of the shares have been allotted in favour of Hon’ble Lieutenant Governor.
