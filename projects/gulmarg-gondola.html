Gulmarg Gondola

Project Introduction

The Gulmarg Gondola is one of the highest cable cars in the world, located in the beautiful region of Gulmarg, Jammu & Kashmir. It offers breathtaking views of the snow-capped mountains and lush green meadows.

Project Features

  • Highest cable car in the world
  • Stunning views of the Himalayas
  • Two-stage ropeway system
  • Modern and safe cabins
  • Popular tourist attraction
